# Introduction 
Helps identify entry points for buying or selling 


# Calculation
$$ MACD = 12Period\_EMA - 26Period\_EMA$$
- Subtract the 26-period EMA from the 12-period EMA
	- EMA is the Exponential Moving Average which gives greater weights to the more recent data points


# Takeaways
- positive value when the lower period crosses the higher period
- negative value vice versa
