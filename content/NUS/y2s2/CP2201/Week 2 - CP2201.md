# Guest Lecturer - Gideon Simons: Ideation

### Takeaways 
- Ideas are cheap: it is free to dream
- Corporate way of ideation
	- Emphasise (with target users - monetary/ emotionally) -> Frame (the problem) -> Ideate (solutions)
- Startup way of ideation
	- explore a space -> fill a gap -> ideate
- Being prepared to pivot your idea is more important than having a good idea
- Sometimes copy pasting and localising is also a good idea! 
	- perspective: the reason why uber had to exit from SG was because they couldn't localise well. Coming frm the US, uber prioritised customer service - drivers did not have the option to reject rides. This business model did not work well. 
- Small change can make a big difference if you understand your customers well:
	- Amazon's $300 million dollar button -> allow users to checkout first before creating an account 
- Try table-top business plan projection sheet
	- Do a conservative one, followed by a worst case scenario
- We are supposed to fall in love with the problem, not the solution!

Idea-centric: Build-Measure-Learn
