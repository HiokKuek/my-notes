- 50/200 for the S&P 500
- For smaller trades, can use 5/15 to trade intra-day
- When choosing different periods, the larger the chart time frame, the stronger and more lasting the golden cross breakout tends to be 
- Golden Cross vs Death Cross 
	- Golden Cross confirms a long-term bull market 
		- Once crossed, long-term MA becomes support
	- Death Cross signals a long-term bear market
		- Once crossed, long-term MA becomes resistance 
	- Signals are more significant when accompanied by **high trading volume**. 

**Key Limitation of Golden Cross: Lagging!**
- Data used to form the charts has already occurred, they indicate past performance. 
- Golden cross is quite good at predicting a large bull market, but there are many times where golden cross fail to manifest.
