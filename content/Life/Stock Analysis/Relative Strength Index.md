# Introduction
Helps traders identify overbought and oversold conditions and generates potential buy and sell signals


- RSI < 30: oversold 
	- Price has fallen too far and too fast
- RSI > 70: overbought
	- Price has risen too quickly
- Divergence between RSI and price movements signals potential reversals

$$
RSI = 100 - \frac{100}{1 + RS}
$$
Where RS = Relative Strength 
- Average gain over a specified period

### Divergence: The strongest RSI signal! 
**Bearish Divergence**
- Price makes a higher high (new high)
- RSI makes a lower high
- Buyers are still pushing price higher, but the momentum is weaker

**Bullish Divergence**
- Price makes a lower low 
- RSI makes a higher low
- Sellers are still pushing the price down, but their strength is fading. The downside momentum is weaker than before
